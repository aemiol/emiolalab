The Emiola laboratory

Microbial Therapeutics Unit

Located at the National Institutes of Health (NIH/NIDCR), the Emiola lab is interested in understanding the function of microbial communities and how they can be explored for therapeutic purposes. Specifically, we seek to use bioinformatic, genomic and molecular biology tools to discover new therapeutic compounds and identify microbial signatures for early detection of diseases.
