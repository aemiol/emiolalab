the barthel laboratory

Welcome to the Barthel Laboratory. Set at the heart of the stunning Sonoran Desert, we study the role of telomere dysfunction in brain cancer (glioma) development and evolution. We are a part of the Cancer and Cell Biology Division at the Translational Genomics Institute (TGen) in Phoenix, AZ.

We are actively looking for new graduate students, postdoctoral fellows and staff to join our team. Please click here for more information.
